# STANDARD 1: Professional Responsibility and Accountability

## Concern
Student displays lack of insight in assessing their own nursing practice

## Strategies & Guidance
Strategies:
- 1:1 meeting with student, preceptor and instructor to discuss the situation
- Ensure the student shares their point of view regarding their nursing practice and what they feel might be areas for improvement
- Suggest counseling and sessions with the Program Head or Student Success Lead
- Encourage self-reflection
- Use a coaching approach to partner with the student and encourage them to self-identify what they need to work on and what they think the best strategies would be.
