# STANDARD 1: Professional Responsibility and Accountability

## Concern
Student displayed inappropriate behaviour towards patients, staff, peers or instructor

## Strategies & Guidance
Refer to BCIT BSN Program Student Guidelines and Procedures
Strategies:
- Suggest student review BCIT Harassment, Discrimination, & Bullying Resources
- Suggest student review the British Columbia College of Nurses & Midwives (BCCNM) Professional Standards
- Suggest student review the BCCNM Professional Responsibility & Accountability Module
- 1:1 meeting with student and instructor outside of the clinical area to discuss the situation - Program Head may be present
